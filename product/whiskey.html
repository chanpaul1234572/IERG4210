Whiskey

Description:

Tennessee whiskey from select barrels (no blending) bottled straight from the barrel. Made with 80% corn, 12% rye and 8% barley then charcoal mellowed. This is an aromatic and surprisingly smooth (considering the proof which can range from 125 up to 140) whiskey, with notes of sweet brown sugar that give way to rich toasted oak and dark peppery spice, best savored neat or with a few cubes of ice. Or, use in a 'Boulevardier' cocktail made with 1 parts Jack Daniel's Single Barrel Barrel Proof, 1 part sweet vermouth and 1 part Campari garnished with a cherry or lemon twist (similar to a Negroni, but substituting whiskey for the gin).

Price: $50
